About Us

The IQ KIDS Early Development School has existed for 5 years in Hamburg. We offer different programs for children aged 6-12. Our team of educators have developed programs based on Montessori, Cecile Lupan, Tony Buzan and adapted them for today's children. The program also includes active development.
